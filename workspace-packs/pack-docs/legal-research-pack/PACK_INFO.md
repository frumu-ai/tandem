# Legal Research Pack

**Description**: A suite of legal documents designed to test Tandem's ability to perform legal analysis, contract review, and case summarization.

**Complexity**: Intermediate-Advanced
**Time Estimate**: 20-25 min
**Best For**: Lawyers, paralegals, legal tech demonstrators

## Contents
- **Inputs**: NDA template, Employment agreement draft, Case notes
- **Outputs**: Legal memo, Risk analysis matrix

## Scenario
You are a junior associate at a law firm. Your partner has handed you a stack of documents related to a new client, "Jones Tech Innovations," and a potential lawsuit involving a former employee. You need to analyze the risks in their employment contracts and summarize the current litigation status.
